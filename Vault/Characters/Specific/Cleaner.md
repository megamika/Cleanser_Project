## Summary

This is the character you play as. He wears a [[Respirator]] most of the time due to his preference of using a fire inflicting weaponary. His goal is to eradicate the [[Infestators]] the same way he did during the [[First plague]].

## Backstory

He was a regular citizen before the [[First plague]] took place. During the [[First plague]] all his friends and family were taken away from him and he lost all hope. He hid far away from all society during that time while his people were taken one by one by the [[Infestators]]. Thankefully the [[Infestators]] has been sucessfully resisted with the help of the [[1st Gen Cleaners]].
