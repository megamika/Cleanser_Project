A humanoid creature. Big head, big arms, small legs. It isn't too mobile and doesn't have a lot of fancy tricks up it's sleve, but it can hit hard with it's hands and can also lunge a small distance with it's mouth.

## Abilities

Bite - a medium range lunge with a bite.
Slash - uses it's hands to slash.
