A stronger, bigger and more dangerous version of a [[Corrupted wolf]]. Most of the time is it accompannied with a number of [[Corrupted wolf]]s. If it is in danger it can call for reinforcements with his voice.

## Abilities

Bite - A short range bite. Used when it is being attacked and it needs to attack first.

Lounge and bite - A long range run and then a jump. It is vulnerable if the attack misses, but does good damage when hit is sucessful.

Call - When in danger will prompt all of the [[Corrupted wolf]]s in his pack to join him and help.
