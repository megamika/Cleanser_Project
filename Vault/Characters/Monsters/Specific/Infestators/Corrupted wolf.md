A dog/wolf like creature. Highly mobile and has a strong bite. It doesn't attack rightaway, but insteads waits for an opportune moment to strike. It will attack you immediately if you decide to attack it first.

## Abilities
Bite - A short range bite. Used when it is being attacked and it needs to attack first.
Lounge and bite - A long range run and then a jump. It is vulnerable if the attack misses, but does good damage when hit is sucessful.
